1. Product Overview

1.1 Vision

HOSTHAVEN is a trust-first accommodation and local services platform, combining:

Airbnb-style visuals & trust (videos, reviews, maps)

OYO-style simplicity & booking flow


The platform focuses on:

Hotels and Homes (booking-based)

Services (manual booking with advance payment)

Temples (content only)

Strong admin control

Vendor hotel management

Real-time inventory (no double bookings)

7. Real-Time Inventory Management (MVP+)

Definition

Inventory tracked per room type, per date

Prevents double bookings


Rules

Temporary lock during payment (5–10 min)

Auto-release on failure/timeout

Confirmed booking permanently reduces availability


Vendor Controls

Set total rooms

Block dates


Admin Controls

Override availability

Cancel bookings
